01 — The practice

Movement that asks for precision, not punishment.

I came to Pilates through ballet — years of learning that real strength is quiet, and that the body works best when every part is doing its share. That's the work I bring into every session: alignment first, breath as the metronome, and a steady focus on the small muscles that hold everything else up.

Sessions are built for one body at a time. Whether you're returning from an injury, standing taller after years at a desk, or chasing a stronger center, we'll start where you are and move with intention. No two programs look the same.
